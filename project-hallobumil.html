Mobile App

Hallobumil Mood Tracker //

A mood tracking feature for Hallobumil — a pregnancy companion app — helping expectant mothers log their daily emotions and understand their emotional well-being throughout pregnancy.

Role
UI/UX Designer
Timeline
2023
Platform
Mobile (Android & iOS)
Tools
Figma
Overview

Hallobumil is a pregnancy companion app that guides expectant mothers through every stage of pregnancy. The Mood Tracker is a feature that lets users record how they feel each day, look back at their emotional patterns over time, and receive relevant content based on their current state.

As the UI/UX Designer, I was responsible for designing the mood tracking experience end-to-end — from the daily check-in flow to the mood history and insight screens.

The Problem
  • Pregnancy comes with significant emotional ups and downs, but users had no simple way to record and reflect on how they were feeling.
  • The app had no input signal about the user's emotional state, so it could not deliver content that matched what mothers were going through.
  • The check-in needed to feel light and comforting — a quick daily ritual, not a clinical form.
The Process
01
Discover

Studied how expectant mothers describe their feelings and reviewed how similar apps handle emotional check-ins.

02
Define

Defined the core flow: a daily check-in that takes seconds to complete, with history and insights as the payoff for consistency.

03
Design

Designed expressive mood options, a calm visual tone, and history views that make emotional patterns easy to see.

04
Deliver

Prototyped the full flow, iterated based on feedback, and handed off the final design to the development team.

The Solution

The final design keeps the daily check-in quick and warm: users pick a mood, optionally add a short note, and they are done. The history view turns those daily entries into a simple emotional timeline, and the app uses the mood data to surface articles and tips that are relevant to how the user actually feels.
